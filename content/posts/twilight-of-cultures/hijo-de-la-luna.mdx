[Hijo de la Luna (Moon's child in Spanish)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VaTb2AtMCfU) is a beautiful song and this is my attempt to make it available for those
that don't speak Spanish. You can always use some translation tool, but until those tools
can provide context here's my humble attempt:
